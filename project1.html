ABOUT US




Consistently, in excess of 11 million visitors visit Foodie eateries around the globe. What's more, they do as
such in light of the fact that our eateries are known for serving astounding, incredible tasting, and reasonable food.
Established in 2012, Foodie is the second-biggest fast-food chain in Asia. The first home of
customary burgers, our promise to premium fixings, signature formulas, and family-accommodating
feasting encounters is the thing that has characterized our image for in excess of 10 fruitful years.
